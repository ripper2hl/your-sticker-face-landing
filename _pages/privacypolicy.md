**Last updated:** February 27, 2026

YourStickerFace (“the App”) is built following a **privacy-by-design** and **offline-first** approach. Its purpose is to anonymize faces locally, without uploading images to external servers.

---

### 1. Information We Collect

YourStickerFace **does not directly collect personally identifiable information**.

The App:

* Does not require user registration.
* Does not collect names, email addresses, or phone numbers.
* Does not create personal user profiles.
* Does not transmit images to external servers.

---

### 2. Local Image Processing

All core features run **entirely on the user’s device**:

* Face detection is performed using on-device machine learning.
* Selected images remain on the device.
* Processed images are not uploaded to any server.

---

### 3. Storage and Export

* Anonymized images are generated as new local files.
* EXIF metadata is removed before export.
* The App does not retain internal copies of processed images.

---

### 4. Device Permissions

The App may request:

* **Access to photos or media files** to allow image selection.
* **Local storage access** to save the final image if the user chooses.

---

### 5. Advertising and Third-Party Services

YourStickerFace may currently be available without advertising.
However, **future versions may include third-party advertising services**, such as ad mediation platforms (e.g., Appodeal).

If advertising is enabled:

* Ad providers may collect technical, non-personal information, including:

  * Advertising identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID).
  * IP address for approximate location.
  * Ad interaction and performance data.
* This data is used solely for ad delivery, analytics, and fraud prevention.

**Ad services do not have access to images or face processing data.**

Data handling is governed by the privacy policies of the respective providers.

---

### 6. Children’s Privacy

YourStickerFace may be used by parents or legal guardians to protect children’s identities.

The App:

* Does not collect children’s personal data.
* Does not transmit images.
* Does not use behavioral or profile-based advertising.

---

### 7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as new features are introduced.
Users are encouraged to review it periodically.
